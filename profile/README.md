**Our Background** <br>
Cube Nation is the largest Cube store in Bangladesh. We started our journey back in August 2016 for the betterment of the Bangladesh cubing community. We sell everything related to speedcubing, puzzle-collecting, accessories, and maintenance items. With having multiple speedcubers in the management team, we provide you with an up-to-date solution to your puzzle-related problems.

**Our History** <br>
CubeNation started in August 2016, by an agonized Cuber who wanted a cubing-related shop, that could give him the necessary specs that he needed at an understandable price. It started with Zero-Investment(allegedly) by taking pre-orders from the customers. Like most of the businesses, it failed miserably on its first attempt. The calculation for the shipment and custom tax was wrong. We had to bear a loss of 50k and suddenly, we were in a very bad position. We thought of going rogue too. Luckily, we received some funding. We tried again after we finally understood the import regulations. Then we dealt with our suppliers in China to get the cubes, created a website, made visiting cards, purchased shipping materials, and other related stuff. Everything was set.
